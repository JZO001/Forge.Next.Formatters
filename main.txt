Add AddForgeFormattersAsTransient DI registration extension

Add a new AddForgeFormattersAsTransient extension method to
ServiceCollectionExtensions that registers all Forge formatters with a
transient service lifetime, complementing the existing scoped and
singleton registration methods. The method registers GZip, XML data,
Brotli (NETCOREAPP only), AES, System.Text.Json and XML SOAP formatters
via AddTransient.

Bump AssemblyVersion from 3.2.1.0 to 3.2.2.0.
